## 2026-06-28 - Avoid Text Opacities Below 50% for Secondary Text on Dark Backgrounds
**Learning:** Using opacity classes like `text-white/30` or `text-white/40` on dark backgrounds (e.g., in WaitlistForm, LaunchTimer, and Footer) results in contrast ratios that fail WCAG AA accessibility standards, making secondary text difficult to read for many users.
**Action:** Always use at least `text-white/50` (or the equivalent `text-muted` if configured properly) for secondary or supplementary text on dark backgrounds to ensure legibility and accessibility compliance. Avoid using opacities below 50% for textual content.
